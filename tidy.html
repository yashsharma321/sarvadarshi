ABOUT SARVADARSHI

Sarvadarshi is a Leading Hindi Monthly News Magazine which is being published from Siliguri & is widely circulated in North Bengal, Sikkim, Bhutan, North-East India and adjoining areas of Nepal & Bihar. Sarvadarshi is a rich and valuable minuscule of one complete month of News & Events. It is equally useful for everyone, especially for the mid-age group, housewives as well as for the general readers who wish to satisfy their hunger for information but don't have time to go through the scores of daily newspapers or weekly magazines. This 32 pages magazine cost Rs. 150/- as annual subscription amount.
